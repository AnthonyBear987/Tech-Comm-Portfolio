Proposal

A proposal to the education system to coordinate with businesses to create a system to house the job application process through classes.

- Google Documents used to create this document.

Safety Document

Lists protection to keep the user safe and aware of common practices when using laser cutters.

- Adobe InDesign used to layout this safety document.

Infographic

A breakdown of laser cutters used throughout the United States. From the types of laser cutters, brands with their respective max cutting potential per materials.

- Adobe Illustrator used to create the layered laser heads and Adobe Indesign used to layout this infographic.

Soldering Training Manual

Worked on a team to design this open source soldering training manual website. Focused on electrical soldering, but more can be added like jewelry soldering and welding. Also created a walkthrough video for electical soldering.

- A Github repository in combination with html coding used to create this soldering website.

Usability Report

Used my team's soldering training manual website to conduct a usability study. I asked my friends to give feedback on the website through the form of a survey. Their feedback gave useful comments on improving the overall site and all attached links at the end of the report.

- A Google Forms survey in combination with Google Docs helped design this report.

Conference Presentation

My conference presentation on job interview tips based on my experience as a mechanical engineering student. I went through the before, during, and after of being interviewed. Focused on what questions asked to me during my interviews in hopes to help out the class.

- Google Slides in combination with my experience and some googling helped design this presentation.
